Subject: SSC submission: trop

Package name: trop
Title: Triple Robust Panel Estimator (TROP)
Authors: Xuanyu Cai (City University of Macau), Wenli Xu (City University of Macau)
Description: Implements the triply robust panel estimator of Athey, Imbens,
  Qu & Viviano (2025) for estimating average treatment effects on the treated
  (ATT) in panel data settings. Combines three estimation strategies (outcome
  modeling via nuclear-norm regularization, propensity weighting, and
  interactive fixed effects) to achieve triply-robust inference. Features
  include leave-one-out cross-validation (LOOCV) for hyperparameter selection,
  cluster bootstrap inference, event-study analysis, multiple hypothesis
  testing correction, and comprehensive postestimation diagnostics.
Required Stata version: 16.0 or later
Required packages: none (self-contained with pre-compiled plugins for
  macOS ARM64, macOS x64, Linux x64, and Windows x64)
Keywords: panel data, causal inference, triple robustness, synthetic control,
  difference-in-differences, LOOCV, interactive fixed effects, ATT,
  treatment effects, nuclear norm
Installation: net install trop, from(https://raw.githubusercontent.com/gorgeousfish/TROP/main/trop_stata)
